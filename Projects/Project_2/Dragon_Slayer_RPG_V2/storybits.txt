Your village has been plagued by the fierce 'Winged Demon' for ages.
Not much is known about this beast since it rarely appears for more
than a few moments while it snatches up its prey, but what is known
is that this 'Demon' must be dispatched. Many attempts have
been made in the past, but things are getting desperate. Trials were
held, and you were chosen. It is up to you to defend your friends and
family from this danger.
